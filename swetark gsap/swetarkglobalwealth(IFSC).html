Swetark Global Wealth (IFSC)

• Swetark Global Wealth (IFSC) •

Empowering Global Investment Excellence

Swetark Global Wealth is the dedicated fund‑management arm of Swetark Group, headquartered in GIFT City IFSC—India’s sole International Financial Services Centre.

As an IFSCA‑licensed Fund Management Entity, we pool subscriber capital into Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs) and deploy it across global markets and India’s dynamic sectors, leveraging GIFT IFSC’s tax‑efficient, multi‑currency framework.
